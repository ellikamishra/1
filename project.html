Ajita Tewari



HI there! I'm Ajita and I love writing.It brings me so much calm and peace.

This site is a collection of all my writings:-Poems,songs,quotes,Thoughts and everything I pen down that strikes my mind!
I write in Hindi,Urdu and a mix of these and other new/old forms of Hindi.

If you too love composing you are at the right place! Checkout my work and feel free to share your ideas,writings and any suggestions.
Most of my compositions are about Romance or Patriotism or Nostalgia(childhood memories you know),so check these out and share them with people fond of writing.



Have a great day!
